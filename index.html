How Does White Label SEO Partnership Work?

How a white label SEO partnership works. Let’s dive into the details.

Step 1: Initial Negotiation and Client Acquisition

In the first stage, represented by Diagram A, the reseller agency engages in negotiations with potential clients. This is where the reseller agency secures clients for various SEO services, such as content creation, link building, or off-page SEO. Once the client subscribes to the reseller agency’s services, we move to the next step.

Find out more about our video: How Does White Label SEO Partnership Work

Step 2: Fulfillment by the White Label SEO Provider

Moving on to Diagram B, this is where the white label SEO provider comes into play. The reseller agency partners with a white label SEO provider who takes on the responsibility of fulfilling the client’s needs. The provider will handle all the deliverables, including SEO tasks, monthly reporting, and any other services included in the reseller’s packages.

Before starting, it’s crucial for the reseller agency and the white label provider to discuss and agree on the packages and services to ensure a smooth working relationship.

Step 3: Reporting and Branding

Once the deliverables are completed, the white label SEO provider sends the reports to the reseller agency. In some cases, the reseller agency might prefer to handle all client communications, including the delivery of reports. However, in a few instances, the white label provider may send the reports directly to the client, but only after rebranding them with the reseller’s logo, color scheme, and fonts to maintain brand consistency.

How Earnings Work

For example, if a client negotiates a monthly budget of $2,000 with the reseller agency, the reseller then finds a white label provider offering the service for $1,000. This leaves the reseller agency with a $1,000 profit. The white label SEO provider does all the work, but the reseller agency receives all the credit, as the service is branded under their name.

Key Parties Involved

There are three main parties in this partnership:

  1. White Label SEO Provider: The company that supplies SEO services.
  2. Reseller Agency: The company that resells these services under their own brand.
  3. Client: The end customer who receives the SEO services from the reseller.

The white label SEO provider is responsible for all the work, following their standard operating procedures (SOPs). They usually have lower rates, especially if they are based in regions like the Philippines, compared to providers in the US, Canada, or Australia.

The Role of the Reseller

The reseller agency is primarily responsible for:

  • Closing deals and acquiring clients.
  • Invoicing and receiving payments.
  • Communicating with clients.
  • Paying the white label SEO provider for their services.

The client, on the other hand, typically believes that the reseller agency is the one doing the work, while in reality, it is the white label SEO provider behind the scenes.

Benefits of White Label SEO

1. Affordable SEO Services

White label providers offer cost-effective SEO services, allowing resellers to earn a profit while both parties benefit from the partnership.

2. Customized SEO Services

White label SEO companies are flexible and can create customized services tailored to the specific needs and preferences of clients. This allows resellers to offer unique solutions to their customers.

3. Analytical Reporting

It's crucial to provide detailed analytical reports to clients. This include baseline data, keyword rankings, traffic metrics, and more. Consistent reporting helps track progress and demonstrates the values of the services provided.

4. Maintaining Brand Identity

Whikte label SEO providers ensure that all deliverables are branded according to the reseller's specifications. This includes using the reseller's logo, fonts, and color schemes to maintain a consistent brand identity.

5. Providing Results to Ensure Repeat Business

Delivering high-quality results is essential for ensuring resellers return for more services. Satisfied resellers are likely to bring in more clients, benefiting both the reseller and the white label SEO provider.
